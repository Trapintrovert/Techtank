Data Analysis (Management) course

  • Underpins almost every service we make, buy and use in buying, selling and providing information.
  • It is about handling, presenting, manipulating, assessing and analysing data, and about making data interoperable, meaning it is usable across users, customers, products and services.

Requirements

1. Work experience - 3 years or more

Course Duration

  • 6 weeks’ timeline in multiple locations across Lagos.

Course Program

Working with data in a way that makes it usable across all public and private entities.

1. Data Cleansing, Linking and Mashing
2. Data-Driven Service Design, Schema and Iteration
3. Data Governance
4. Data Protection
5. Data Quality
6. Data Visualization for the Web
7. Labelling and Cataloguing
8. Open Data and Standards
9. Stakeholder Management
10. Statistical and Probability Modelling
11. Use of Big Data
