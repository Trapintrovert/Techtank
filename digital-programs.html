DIGITAL TRAINING

We provide and enable digital learning experiences with a difference in the ecosystem. Our method of delivery bridges the gap by addressing the digital challenges with competency-based practical work environment.

Unlike traditional training workshops, TechTank’s program focuses on agile learning, exposure, experience and collaboration across business users, consumers, socio-economic outlook and growth.

Our core value ensures people achieve digital competency, inclusion and trends required for varied socio-economic professions and markets.

“Digital skills are any skills related to being digitally literate, which is the ability to find, evaluate, utilize, share and create content using information technologies. The information technologies include elementary knowledge and understanding of content, users, designs, platforms, software and their interaction with the internet”.
