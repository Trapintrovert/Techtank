Performance Analysis course is a process

  • Of continual iteration and close measurement to see what needs improvement and to investigate ways of improving the service.
  • That involves generating new and useful information and translating it into actions. Data underpins almost every service we make, buy and use in service and solutions.

Requirements

1. Work experience - 3 years or more

Course Duration

  • 6 weeks’ timeline in multiple locations across Lagos.
  • Includes training, practice and development

Course Program

Performance analysis involves measuring and interpreting data to see where and how a service can be improved.

Interpreting and using performance data to continuously improve services.

1. A/B and Multivariate Testing
2. Advances in Analytics Technologies
3. Analysing Data
4. Data Security and Privacy
5. Manage and Develop Analytics Architecture
6. Measurement
7. Performance Platform
8. Provide Business Insight from Analysis
9. Representing the User
10. Support Development Teams to Capture Data
11. Synthetic Data
12. Understand Business Processes
13. Use Data to Understand Content
13. User Research
