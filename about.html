TechTank’s vision

Our vision is to become the recognized EdTech leader as the Pan-African Centre of digital ‘solutions, education, professionals and technology consulting’ ecosystem focused on job creation and human capital development, whilst providing digital skills training, innovative programs, research and development services.

TechTank’s mission

1. To address social economic challenges and support opportunities leading to competency-based improvement across all sectors.
2. Tackle the barriers limiting people's digital capabilities, such as lack of quality access, skills, and confidence.
3. Increase digital inclusion by offering practical work-based related programs and experience
4. Establish a social agenda that alerts businesses to changes in their service operations, whilst supporting shared inclusion.

“Every element of our lives revolves around technology. Without tech, people lack the optimism to achieve their goals and objectives. It doesn't necessarily mean being an expert coder, but to be proficient and familiarize oneself with digital tools to become comfortable with them.”

TechTank’s values

1. Passionate about customer and human capital development
2. Own and act on behalf of the company through short-term results and long-term value
3. Innovate through simplified processes across team members.
4. Learning and seeking new possibilities to explore and improve
5. Deliver high standard quality products, services and processes.
6. Think big by communicating bold direction that inspires results.
7. Dive deep into details to deliver key inputs and outcomes
