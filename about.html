TechTank Profile

Founded to support the 4th industrial revolution, TechTank is profoundly Nigerian and predominantly African. TechTank is the trading name of Tech Think Tank Initiative Limited RC 1529576.

TechTank community is driven by passionate people thriving to create a better environment for us all through learning, research, development and innovation. We are energetic, lively, active and agile. We come from a diverse variety of disciplines from technology to science, arts to entertainment, engineering to design and are open to everyone regardless of their background.

Our vision is to become the recognized EdTech leader, providing practical hands-on experience and real-time project exposure to enable digital inclusion for all by:

1. Addressing social economic challenges and supporting opportunities leading to competency-based improvement across all sectors.
2. Tackling the barriers limiting people's digital capabilities, such as lack of quality access, skills, and confidence.
3. Increasing digital inclusion through hands-on experience and exposure to real-time program.
4. Establishing a social agenda that alerts businesses to changes in their service operations, whilst supporting shared digital inclusion.
